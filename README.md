## What Is action.rp?

**action.rp** is an AI roleplay platform where users can create, share, discover, and chat with custom characters.

The goal is simple:

> Make AI roleplay feel more alive, more personal, and more community-driven.

Instead of only chatting with random bots, users can build their own characters, customize their experience, explore public bots made by others, and shape the vibe of every conversation.

Whether you want a dramatic fantasy scene, a chaotic school RP, a soft romance setup, a horror mystery, a comfort character, or just a bot that actually reacts to the moment, action.rp is designed to give people a place to create and roleplay freely.

---

## Features

### AI Character Chats

Chat with AI characters in immersive roleplay conversations.

Characters can respond with mood, emotion, narration, dialogue, and scene awareness. The chat experience is built to feel more like entering a story than sending messages to a plain chatbot.

Users can choose response styles such as:

- **Short** - faster, cleaner replies
- **Balanced** - natural roleplay length
- **Detailed** - richer and more descriptive responses
- **Auto** - lets the AI decide based on the scene

Some advanced response modes are designed for supporter or premium-style access.

---

### Create Your Own Characters

Users can create their own bots with:

- Character name
- Description
- Greeting or opening scene
- Definition and personality
- Categories
- Visibility options
- Character image
- Optional recommended song

Characters can be public, unlisted, or private depending on how the creator wants to share them.

---

### AI-Assisted Character Creation

action.rp includes an AI character creation flow where users can answer a few short questions and let AI help generate a more detailed character.

The idea is to make character creation easier for everyone, even users who are not used to writing long character definitions.

Instead of staring at a blank form, users can give the app a simple idea and turn it into something more complete.

---

### Community Profiles

Every creator has a profile page.

Profiles are designed to feel familiar and social, with a layout inspired by modern profile-based apps.

Users can see:

- Public bots
- Liked characters
- Profile picture
- Bio
- Username
- Badges
- Public stats

The goal is to make creators feel like real members of the community, not just anonymous bot uploaders.

---

### Badges And Community Identity

action.rp supports profile badges for special users and community roles.

Examples include:

- Early User
- Closed Beta Tester
- Moderator
- Supporter-style badges

Users can choose a display badge, while their full badge collection can still appear on their profile.

This helps make the community feel alive and gives early supporters a permanent identity inside the app.

---

### Interests And Discovery

Users can choose interests so the app can recommend characters more intelligently.

Discovery sections include:

- **For You**
- **Trending**
- **New**
- **Featured**

The For You system is designed to prioritize the user's interests while still keeping the character pool open, so discovery does not become too narrow.

---

### Chat Customization

Users can personalize the chat screen with different visual styles.

Customization includes:

- Themes
- Chat colors
- Font selection
- Bubble size
- Background options
- Mood glow effects
- Pride Month theme
- Custom local background images

The app tries to keep the roleplay experience personal, because different stories need different moods.

---

### Mood Glow System

The AI can detect the overall emotional mood of a scene and reflect it visually.

For example:

- Tense scenes can glow purple
- Angry scenes can lean red
- Softer scenes can feel warmer
- Happier scenes can shift brighter

The mood itself is not shown as text in the chat. It is used quietly in the UI to make the conversation feel more atmospheric.

---

### Recommended Songs

Characters can include a recommended Spotify song.

Instead of forcing music playback, the app can show a compact Spotify-style recommendation card so users can open the track externally.

This gives creators another way to define the feeling of a character or story.

---

### On-Device Message Enhancement

action.rp experiments with local AI features for enhancing user messages directly on the device.

The idea is simple:

A user writes something short like:

```txt
*he smirks* what?
```

The local enhancer can help expand only the action part into something more expressive while keeping the user's original message intact.

This feature is experimental and depends on device performance.

---

### Discord Supporter Integration

The app is connected with the community Discord.

Supporter-style features can be unlocked through Discord roles, helping connect the app and the community together.

The long-term idea is to reward active community members without making everything feel isolated inside the app.

## Why action.rp Exists

AI roleplay apps are powerful, but many of them feel closed, limited, or disconnected from the people who create the characters.

action.rp is built around a different idea:

> The community should not just consume characters.  
> The community should create the world.

The app is designed so users can make characters, share them, build profiles, earn badges, customize chats, and participate in shaping the platform.

It is still early, but the direction is clear: a roleplay app that feels social, alive, and creator-friendly.

---

## Roadmap

Planned and experimental features include:

### Better Character Discovery

More advanced recommendation systems, better filtering, and improved category support.

### More Community Tools

Creator profiles, badges, moderation tools, public collections, and stronger Discord integration.

### Improved AI Responses

Better roleplay pacing, smarter scene awareness, flexible response length, and improved character consistency.

### Safer Character Moderation

Tools to detect and manage unsafe or unwanted public content.

### Web Version

A web version is being explored so users can browse and interact with action.rp from desktop as well as mobile.

### Premium And Supporter Features

Some advanced customization and AI features may become premium features in the future.

Early supporters and eligible Discord role holders may keep access to certain features depending on the final system.

## Privacy And Safety

action.rp is built with privacy and user control in mind.

Planned and existing safety-related systems include:

- Legal consent flow
- Account deletion
- Profile controls
- Character visibility controls
- Local-only customization data where possible
- Community moderation tools
- Clearer public/private content separation

The goal is to keep the app fun and creative while respecting users and their data.
